About

Hello and welcome to my site! I'm a motivated and creative student with a strong foundation in science and technology and a deep interest in international politics and foreign languages. Seeking a highly challenging university experience to deepen my knowledge and expand my horizons to prepare for a career in the technology sector

Software Developer & Entrepreneur

My early inspiration to study computer science was stories of startup unicorns, but revelations about the dark side of tech and “anything for clicks”--Data Mining (Facebook), Privacy (TikTok), Addiction (Snapchat), Disinformation (Twitter)--has pushed me down a new path. I am now inspired by achievements in space travel, synthetic biology, and autonomy, which advance the human condition, but even these are at risk as well. I have a vision to step into this domain, applying my passion for technology with my passion for foreign languages to compete against malign influences, especially those from Russia and China, and drive towards secure and ethical technology
